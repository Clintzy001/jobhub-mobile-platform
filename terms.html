SECURITY TERMS

Safe Trading Guidelines for Kano

01. Payment Policy

KHUB strictly advises NEVER to send money (including commitment fees or delivery charges) before seeing, inspecting, and testing the physical item.

02. Meeting Points

Always meet sellers in Public Locations within Kano (e.g., Shoprite, Ado Bayero Mall, or busy plazas). Avoid private residences or isolated areas for first-time transactions.

03. Verification

It is the buyer's responsibility to verify the quality and authenticity of the product. Once payment is made, KHUB cannot mediate in disputes regarding item condition.

04. Fraud Reporting

If a seller seems suspicious or demands advance payment, report their WhatsApp number to KHUB Support immediately to have them blacklisted.
